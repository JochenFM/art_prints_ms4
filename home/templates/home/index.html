About this shop

This site offers a fine selection of original art prints from around the globe. The focus is on paper collectibles from around 1900, such as advertising cards developed from around 1905 by Swiss editor and entrepreneur Karl Wilhelm Bührer (1861-1917). These so-called "Mono cards" or "Monos" are currently the bulk of what is on offer, but I hope to be able to display more vintage art posters and prints, and stamps in the near future. You can also contribute to our blog and help our community of paper art traders and affictionados grow.
